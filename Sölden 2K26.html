🎒 Skiing Equipment Checklist

  • Ski jacket (waterproof, breathable)
  • Ski pants (insulated or shell with layers)
  • Thermal base layers (merino wool preferred)
  • Ski socks (moisture-wicking, multiple pairs)
  • Ski gloves + spare liner gloves
  • Ski goggles (multiple lens options)
  • Ski helmet (mandatory in many areas)
  • Sunglasses (glacier-rated UV protection)
  • High SPF sunscreen (mountain sun is intense)
  • Lip balm with SPF protection
  • Après-ski clothes & comfortable boots
  • Warm hat for non-skiing activities

📝 Daily Preparation Checklist

  • Check weather forecast and dress appropriately
  • Apply sunscreen (face, neck, hands)
  • Ensure ski pass is easily accessible
  • Carry emergency contact information
  • Plan meeting points and times with group
  • Make lunch reservations if needed
  • Check equipment condition (edges, bindings)
  • Know location of first aid stations
  • Carry small backpack with essentials
  • Stay hydrated - bring water bottle

🎿 Pro Skiing Tips

⏰ Timing:
First lifts = fresh snow, fewer crowds. Lunch 11:30-12:30 to avoid rush.
🗺️ Navigation:
Download offline slope maps. Mark meeting points on your phone.
💧 Hydration:
Altitude + dry air = dehydration. Drink water regularly, limit alcohol.
📷 Photography:
Best light: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM. Protect phone battery in cold weather.
